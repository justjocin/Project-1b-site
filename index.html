About

Silas Thomas (born May 17, 2003), popularly known as Justjocin, is an American recording artist and producer from Edgard, Louisiana. He goes by $igh when he produces. Being the grandson of a choir-enjoying grandmother, he grew up in a musical household. His father, Tji Thomas, is said to be his main inspiration for his musical sound. His father was a big trap fan as Silas grew up, and would play a multitude of artists around him; this includes, Gucci Mane, Rick Ross, Young Jeezy, and even Waka Flocka.
